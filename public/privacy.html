Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Harp Diem is a free, client-side web app for visualizing harmonica scales. It runs entirely in your browser, and is designed to collect as little as possible.

What we don't collect

Harp Diem has no user accounts, no backend server storing your data, no advertising, and no third-party tracking or advertising cookies. We do not sell or share personal data — we don't have any to sell.

Microphone

The built-in tuner uses your microphone only while you turn it on. Audio is analyzed in real time inside your browser to detect pitch. It is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere. When you stop the tuner or leave the page, microphone access ends.

Local storage

We save a couple of small preferences in your browser's local storage — your light/dark theme choice and your reference tuning pitch (A4 Hz). These never leave your device, and you can clear them anytime through your browser settings.

Error reporting

To keep the app reliable, Harp Diem may send anonymous technical error reports (such as a stack trace and browser type) to an error-monitoring service if a crash occurs. These reports contain diagnostic information only and are used solely to fix bugs.

Hosting

The site is served over HTTPS from Amazon Web Services (S3 + CloudFront). Standard server logs (such as IP address and request time) may be processed by AWS to deliver content and protect against abuse, per AWS's privacy practices.
